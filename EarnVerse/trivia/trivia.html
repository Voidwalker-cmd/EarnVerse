DeFi, or Decentralized Finance, is a financial system built on blockchain technology that provides financial services like lending, borrowing, and trading directly between users (peer-to-peer) without relying on traditional intermediaries like banks or brokers. It uses smart contracts to automate financial agreements and transactions, making services open, global, and generally more accessible while aiming to reduce costs and transaction times.
